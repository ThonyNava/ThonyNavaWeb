Hello, I'm Thony

I'm an artistic technologist that wants to create technology for making humankind a multiplanetary spice. I am into multiple spectrums of creative creation processes, so I am life long learner, pushing the boundaries of my knowledge.

Strategy

Understanding the intrinsic principles behind things is a key factor for succeeding in any project that aims to bring to reality a new idea. I start by identifying theses principles. From them on I build up a plan constituted by short term goals.

Execution

Planning only makes sense when actions go after it. Execution done right is a disciplined process, a logical set of connected activities to make the strategy work. I set clear priorities, collect and analyze data, and evaluate the strategies.

Extra mile

No solution is too good for lasting forever. Once you understand the problem you are pretending to solve, the evolution and scalability of projects come. Analyzing and starting over from scratch for new solutions is part of the workflow.
